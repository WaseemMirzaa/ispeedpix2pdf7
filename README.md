# ispeedpix2pdf7

*A fast and simple Flutter app to convert gallery images into high-quality PDFs.*

---

## Overview

**ispeedpix2pdf7** is a Flutter-powered PDF creator that lets users quickly transform their gallery images into clean, compressed, and shareable PDFs.
With support for **three PDF layout modes** across **portrait** and **landscape** orientations, it offers full control over how images are arranged and displayed.

The app includes **custom quality settings**, **compression tools**, and instant options to **save or share** PDFs.
All features are available under a **one-time lifetime subscription of just $1.99**.
